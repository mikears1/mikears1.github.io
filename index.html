I conducted a comprehensive supply chain analysis for HEB, leveraging Python, Excel, and Power BI to optimize distribution efficiency and measure environmental impact.
Using a custom Python pipeline with pandas, NumPy, and geospatial libraries like GeoPandas, Shapely, and Contextily, I calculated route-level carbon emissions based on Haversine distance, truck type, and payload.
I visualized emissions across distribution centers and retail markets with interactive maps and aggregated the data to generate actionable insights.
I also created KPIs and dashboards in Power BI to compare emissions and transportation distances across different distribution scenarios, enabling data-driven decisions for sustainability and operational improvements.
